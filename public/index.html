How to use this website

Click the buttons on the right to learn about verbs, nouns, and adjectives. Each button will give you examples of each type of word and what they mean.

Click on one of the madlib buttons to start filling out your madlib!

information about verbs

a word used to describe an action, state, or occurrence, and forming the main part of the predicate of a sentence, such as hear, become, happen..

information about noun

a word (other than a pronoun) used to identify any of a class of people, places, or things ( common noun ), or to name a particular one of these ( proper noun ).
